3D World

Publication Design

Project Summary

The objective for this project was to design a magazine spread for the 3D World magazine company. 3D world is an international informative magazine for 3D artist, animators, and videogame designers. I designed a cover, table of contents and one spread for the latest issue featuring a game called God of War Ragnarok. The design follows the existing qualities of the original 3D World magazines with an improvement in layout and design.
