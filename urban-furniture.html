Urban Furniture

Branding

Project Summary

Urban Furniture is a premier furniture store located in Brooklyn, New York, offering a collection of modern and contemporary pieces for any living space. The design for the brand incorporates clean lines and minimalist aesthetics from the furniture collection. The brand caters to customers who are looking for sophisticated and modern interiors. Their selection boasts a range of items crafted from high-quality materials, showcasing neutral colors and curved forms.

Brief

The objective of this project is to design a brand identity and stationery items for Urban Furniture. Deliverables will include business cards, letterheads, and envelopes. The stationery should reflect the design and quality of the brand's products. The final products will be used for both internal communication and external correspondence.
