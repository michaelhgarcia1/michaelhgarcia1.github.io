Oracle Hear

Product Packaging, Branding

Project Summary

The project examines issues of design sustainability & system design within the context of package design including form and graphics. I was tasked with redesigning an existing product that was using excessive and wasteful materials such as plastic and papers. I researched materials and designed a right-sized solution for the product. I also redesigned product graphics for enhanced shelf presence and marketability.

Brief

Oracle Hear is a leading manufacture of innovative hearing aids, dedicated to improving the quality of life for individuals with hearing impairments. The company focuses on cutting-edge technology, personalized solutions, and exceptional customer care. The company was founded in 2015 in Fort Lauderdale Florida.
