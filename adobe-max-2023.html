Adobe MAX 2023

Visual Design

Project Summary

This project was a design poster for the Adobe MAX 2023 conference held at the Los Angeles Convention Center. The design is inspired by elements of creativity such as interactivity with objects and materials. The paint splatter in the center of the composition represents creativity of artists who use Adobe products. 3D software was used to create the paint splatter and "MAX" letters This poster was selected for the Annual Juried Exhibition at Florida Atlantic University, and was exhibited in the Metro Lab Gallery in downtown Fort Lauderdale.

Dimensions: 24x36 in
Medium: Digital Print

Juried Exhibition

My design was selected to be showcased in the Florida Atlantic University Juried Student Exhibition. Curated by Richard Rodriguez.

Process

Model

For the 3D model I created a particle simulation inside of Blender and then transformed it into a mesh. I used the type tool for the letters and extruded the geometry for more depth. The materials I used consisted of a glass texture I created in the shader editor and applying a lower roughness on the paint splatter for reflectivity. For lighting I used an HDRI image and an area light for a more dramatic effect.
