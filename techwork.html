TechWork networking platform

Web, 3D, Branding

Project Summary

TechWork is a networking platform that connects tech enthusiasts and professionals from all areas of the tech industry. The site features a landing page that provides insights into the platform, a call to action, and a news feed on trending topics.

Live site

Design

The design uses animated 3D models for a more interactive and unique experience that keeps the user's attention. The colors and typography chosen give a playful and inviting feeling that reflects a sense of community. There is a futuristic quality to the site, as the images and models used have a minimalist design that makes for a modern website.

Models

The models used in this project where created in Spline 3D and embedded as code. The scene is meant to represent the many different sectors within the tech industry. Computers, servers, internet search icons, and a large machine that represents innovation.
