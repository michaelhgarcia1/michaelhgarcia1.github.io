BFA Invitation

Exhibition Invitation

Project Summary

This project was an invitation design for the OFFSETÂ BFA exhibition held by the graduating class of BFA Graphic design majors from Florida Atlantic University. Each student was assigned to design an invitation that would be used to invite local design professionals to attend the student exhibition. Out of a class of 20 students, my invitation was chosen to be sent to over 1000 designers, agencies, and creative directors in South Florida.

Print Specs

Job: FAU Spring 2024 Exhibition Invitation
Quantity: 1500
Cover: 80#C Corniche Velvet
Size: 12x6 inch flat, 6x6 inch folded
Ink: 4 color process both sides
Special: die cut, bleeds all sides, heavy
ink coverage on one side

Design

The design for the invitation was inspired by gradient design trends and y2k aesthetics with the usage of typography and colors. The square die cut was inspired by the name Offset as it is placed offset from the center of the design.
